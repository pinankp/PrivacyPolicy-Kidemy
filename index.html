1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Kidemy does not collect, store, share, or process any personal or sensitive user data from users of any age, including children.

2. Compliance with Applicable Laws

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

No personal information is collected or processed; therefore, GDPR obligations are inherently met.

CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

Users’ personal data is not collected, sold, or shared. Therefore, CCPA consumer rights are automatically respected.

COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act)

Kidemy is designed for children and does not collect personal information from children under 13.

Kidemy follows Google Play requirements for child safety, including no data collection and no third-party SDKs that track user activity.

Kidemy does not collect or process any type of user information. Specifically, we do not collect:

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

No advertisements or external analytics/monetization tools are used in the application.

Kidemy does not integrate AdMob, Firebase, Facebook SDK, or any external tracking provider.

5. Children’s Privacy

Kidemy is safe for children. No information is collected intentionally or automatically. If any personal information is accidentally submitted, we will delete it immediately upon notification.
